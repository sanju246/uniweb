Professor Dr. S. M. Ikhtiar Alam

Professor, IBA, JU

×

Professor Dr. S. M. Ikhtiar Alam

Professor Dr. Md. Motaher Hossain received his Doctor of philosophy degree from University of Helsinki, Finland in consumer economics. He obtained Master of Business Administration from L. N. M. Institute of Economic Development and Social Change, Magadh University, India. He completed Bachelor of Science (Hons.) from Aligarh Muslim University, India. As a postgraduate researcher Dr. Hossain has worked in various research projects with Professor Visa Heinonen, Professor Matti Peltonen, and Professor John Sumelius of the University of Helsinki. He has worked as a member of the ‘Transformation of the Consumer Society (KUMU)’ research group and ‘Sustainable Agriculture and Rural Development Research Group (SARG)’ of University of Helsinki. Dr. Hossain published many articles as well as worked as a referee of different national and international journals. He attended many international conferences and presented research articles in the United Kingdom, China, Finland, and Japan. He has successfully supervised (degree awarded) two PhD students and, at present, supervising a few MPhil and PhD students. In addition to that, every year he supervises several BBA and MBA students.

Research Interests

  • Consumption
  • Consumer Economics
  • CSR
