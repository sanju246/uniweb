Overview of Student Clubs

The student clubs at Jahangirnagar University (JU) create an active and dynamic space for students to explore their passions, develop new skills, and form lasting connections. These clubs cover a wide variety of fields, including academics, culture, sports, and community service. By participating in workshops, competitions, events, and social activities, students not only enhance their personal growth and leadership abilities but also contribute to a lively campus atmosphere. These clubs offer an invaluable platform for students to collaborate, innovate, and make the most of their university experience.

Club Activities

Business Case Competition 2024

A challenging event hosted by the IBA Business Club where teams competed to solve real-world business cases. The event featured thought-provoking discussions and dynamic presentations, creating lasting memories of teamwork and problem-solving.

Inter-Club Cultural Fest 2025

The annual Cultural Fest celebrated by students, filled with vibrant music, dance performances, and artistic exhibitions. This event allowed participants to express their creativity and unite through art and culture, creating fond memories that will last a lifetime.

Sports Tournament Week

A week-long sports extravaganza where students competed in football, cricket, and basketball. The spirit of healthy competition, camaraderie, and teamwork marked the event, leaving behind memories of thrilling matches and victorious moments.
