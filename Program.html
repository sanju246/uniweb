Program

Institute of Business Administration, Jahangirnagar University (IBA-JU) offers three academic programs – BBA at undergraduate level, regular MBA & Weekend MBA at postgraduate level – and two research based programs, M.Phil. and Ph.D. at higher study level.

BBA Program

IBA-JU offers a 4-year Bachelor of Business Administration program

MBA Program

IBA-JU offers a one year’s full-time Master of Business Administration Program for the fresh BBA graduates from this institute.

WMBA Program

IBA-JU offers a professional at the post graduate level on Master of Business Administration on the weekends.
