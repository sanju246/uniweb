Department Notices

Upcoming Workshop on AI

Join us for a workshop on Artificial Intelligence on December 20, 2024. Learn from experts in the field.

Location: Conference Room 101, 10 AM. This workshop will cover the latest trends in AI, machine learning, and their real-world applications. Experts from the field will conduct sessions.

Semester Break Announcement

The department will be closed for the semester break from January 1st to January 10th. Please plan accordingly.

The department will be closed during the semester break. All classes and office hours will resume on January 11th. Please ensure all your academic and administrative tasks are completed before the break.

Guest Lecture on Quantum Computing

A guest lecture on Quantum Computing will be held on December 25, 2024. Don't miss out!

Location: Auditorium, 2 PM. The guest lecture will cover the basics of quantum computing, its potential applications, and how it is revolutionizing the tech industry. Open to all students and faculty.

Annual Sports Day Announcement

Our Annual Sports Day will be held on January 15, 2025. All students are encouraged to participate.

Events will include track races, volleyball, basketball, and more. Registration starts on January 5th, and all students are encouraged to sign up to participate in various events. Stay tuned for further details!

New Semester Registration

Registration for the new semester begins on January 5, 2025. Make sure to complete your registration before January 12.

Students must complete their course registration and fee payment before January 12, 2025. Late registration will incur additional fees, so please ensure all forms are submitted on time.
