About Institute of Business Administration

The Institute of Business Administration, Jahangirnagar University (IBA-JU)**, established in 1991 and
elevated to an institute in 2009, is a pioneer in offering Bangladesh's first Bachelor of Business
Administration (BBA) program. Renowned for its academic excellence and dynamic faculty with global
teaching and research experience, IBA-JU fosters a competitive learning environment on its picturesque
campus. The institute empowers students from diverse backgrounds to excel in national and international
business arenas, producing skilled graduates who significantly contribute to society.

we are committed to fostering a vibrant academic community and empowering our students to reach their full potential.
