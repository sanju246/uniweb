About

Institute of Business Administration,
Jahangirnagar University (IBA-JU) is the pioneer institute in offering Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) degree in Bangladesh.
In 1991, it started its journey as a department and, it gained the pride to be the institute in 2009.
With its successful operation of the undergraduate and post-graduate programs,
a number of efficient and skillful business graduates have been contributing to society.
IBA-JU offers the high standard of academic environment in a spectacularly beautiful campus.
The key strength of the institute lies on the well-qualified and dedicated faculty members and staff.
Many of the academicians have rich and dynamic research and teaching experiences in different renowned universities of the world. Further, the institute provides the platform to the students of diverse social, cultural and economic backgrounds to unleash their potentiality.
It facilitates students to be competent for the business challenges at the national level and to be capable of building career at the international level.
